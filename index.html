Introduction

The Arcades is a small Minecraft minigames server made differently from what you're used to play.

I'm getting closer to a complete redesign from scratch of everything on the server, offering a game design so different and flexible, to let development be open and inspiring for: (also big) new ideas & integrations through the main system and other games also made by me.

This is the work of a single person sometime helped by his friends, that built and developed almost everything on the server:

  • Inspired by Hypixel's magnitude and the Roblox idea to serve a vast variety of contents, in a built-in catalog interface;
  • Powered by the desire of making something that's not just entertainment;
  • Using every Minecraft 1.8 available asset like titles, heads, entities, and tricks with blocks or other assets to achieve a different result.

This server is running on Minecraft version 1.8.8, as it is the best version for minigames: it is equipped with enough assets, and due to the old combat system that is preferred by the players in pvp minigames.

I'm not searching for staff members.

Incomplete

This project requires a huge amount of time and resources!
I'm working on this since May 2023 and it's still really far from being a playable server!

I'm constantly improving the server base I called Server Core. It's the Main Plugin that handles and connect everything together. Althought I'm changing this base so often that brakes the entire server and the minigames. This discourages me to complete a minigame development, before being sure the base has everything I need and, it's all well made to allow future ideas/changes or issues.
